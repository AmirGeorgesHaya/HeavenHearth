Meet the Entertainers!

Freddy Fazbear!

Schedule:

Available on Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursdays

Get to know him!

Freddy is a well-known animatronic that can dance, sing, and serve tables as well!
Popularized by the 80s, Freddy Fazbear has won the hearts of millions of children around the world.
He is the perfect entertainer if you think about bringing kids to Heaven Hearth. Who knows, maybe Freddy
will bring along his friends to celebrate special occasions such as Christmas or Easter. If you order a pizza
note that Freddy himself will serve your table since it's his favorite dish in the whole restaurant!
A special meal called the Freddy Ready Burger is only available at his showings!

Caution!
Kids should be wary of the Animatronic's head.

*Ask more to your local server for the special meals!

Jigglypuff!

Schedule:

Available on Fridays

Get to know her!

In collaboration with the Pokemon Company, this Jigglypuff run autonomously and
brought to life thanks to modern animatronic technology. Her singing skills are unmatched and push
the limits of what animatronics can do. She is perfect for setting a pleasant, calming, and nice atmosphere.
A special drink called: Pink Pink Galore is served only on Jigglypuff's showings to celebrate such an amazing
balloon animatronic, so don't miss it!

*Ask more to your local server for the special meals!

Kass!

Schedule:

Available on Saturdays and Sundays

Get to know him!

Partnered up with Nintendo, The greatest musician from Zelda: Breath of the Wild is brought to life!
Thanks to our amazing engineering, Kass is an amazing entertainer for setting the atmosphere of the room.
While Jigglypuff's singing may be her strong suit. Kass's accordion remains unmatched for its amazing sound.
He may not be much of a singer, but his musical expertise outweighs all of it and makes this singing animatronic
a must-have! He accepts any requests and can play any music asked by the clients.
A special meal called: Victorian Scorpion is served only on his showings.

*Ask more to your local server for the special meals!
